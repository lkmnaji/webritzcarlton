MEETINGS AND CONFERENCES AT THE RITZ-CARLTON

BEYOND THE

BOARDROOM

At The Ritz-Carlton, meetings are more than gathering around a conference table:
They’re joining colleagues to plant trees in Lake Tahoe, taking the afternoon to surf
in Bali or bonding with teammates in a state-of-the-art kitchen in Cleveland. Using
the destination as their foundation, meeting experiences are crafted with beautiful
venues, the latest technology, locally inspired catering and highly personalized
service.


DISCOVER OUR NEWEST MEETING VENUES

Each destination celebrates its surroundings with iconic views and locally inspired experiences.


THE RITZ-CARLTON PERTH

Indoor and outdoor meeting venues overlook the city skyline and Swan River, and catering celebrates Western Australian ingredients.

THE RITZ-CARLTON,PUNE

A reflection of India’s grace and grandeur, meeting venues include a large ballroom and outdoor spaces for breaks and receptions.

THE RITZ-CARLTON,NANJING

In the heart of the business district, this hotel’s event spaces feature panoramic city views. The ballroom includes a rooftop terrace.
